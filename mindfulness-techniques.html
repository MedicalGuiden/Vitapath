Practical Techniques for Everyday Mindfulness

Mindfulness is the act of being consciously present in the moment without judgment. It helps reduce stress, improve focus, and promote mental clarity.

1. Breathing Meditation

Sit quietly, focus on your breath, and gently bring your mind back when it wanders. This simple technique increases awareness and relaxation.

2. Body Scan

Lie down or sit comfortably. Slowly scan through your body, from head to toe, noting tension or discomfort without trying to fix it.

3. Mindful Walking

Pay attention to each step, the feeling of your feet touching the ground, your breathing, and the rhythm of your body as you walk.
